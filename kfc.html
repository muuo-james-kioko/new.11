KENTUCKY FRIED CHICKEN

KFC Corporation, also known as KFC, is an American fast food restaurant that specialises in fried chicken. Its headquartes are in LOUISVILLE, Kentucky. It is the second largest restaurant chain in the world with over 30,00 locations in globarly.

KFC was founded by Colonel Harland Sanders(1890-1980),an entrepreneur who started selling fried chicken by his road side restaurant in Corbin,Kentucky, guring the great depresssion. Sanders identified the potential of restaurant franchising concept, and the first francise was opened in Salt Lake city,Utah, in 1952. KFC popularised chicken in the fast food industry, diversifying the market by challenging the established dominance of the hamburger. By branding himself as " COLONEL SANDERS ", Harland became a prominent figure in the American history. However the rapid franchising overwhelmd the already aging Sanders , and in 1964, he sold it to a group of investors
